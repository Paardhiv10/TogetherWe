Indian Government Schemes for Health Care

Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana

Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana is a government scheme aimed at providing health insurance coverage to economically vulnerable families. It covers up to Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization expenses.

Learn More

National Health Mission

National Health Mission is a government program aimed at improving the availability and accessibility of quality healthcare services in rural and urban areas. It focuses on maternal and child health, communicable and non-communicable diseases, and health infrastructure development.

Learn More

Ayushman Bharat

Ayushman Bharat is a government initiative aimed at providing universal health coverage to all citizens of India. It includes two components: Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana, which provides health insurance coverage, and Health and Wellness Centers, which provide comprehensive primary healthcare services.
